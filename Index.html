Sea Salt

Sea salt is harvested from ocean water, which can contain pollutants and impurities. In contrast, Nutri Green Salt is sourced from the pristine Salicornia plant, ensuring a clean and healthy product.

Bamboo Salt

Bamboo salt is made by roasting sea salt in bamboo tubes, which can impart unique flavors but also alters its nutritional composition. Nutri Green Salt retains its natural state, preserving its health benefits.

Rock Salt

Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and can often contain impurities and environmental contaminants. Nutri Green Salt is organic and free from such concerns, ensuring purity and safety.
